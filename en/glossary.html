Glossary

-Angels: In the Neon Genesis Evangelion universe, they are huge divines creatures, menacing the world and the humanity. To counter these monsters, the humanity sends them the Evangelions, to destroy them and saving what is left of humans.

-EVA or Evangelion: A Giant robot, or Mecha, from the Neon Genesis Universe. They need to be piloted by someone to be efficient, it is at the same time a weapon and a living being.

-Ikigami: A death notice sent to people infected by a mysterious vaccine. The victims are informated one day before the expiration date of the death notice, so they may spent some time with their close friends and family.

-Dystopy: Opposite of the Utopy, it is a pessimistic future where the society is fading ou has already fading away, these worlds are often impossible to save or repair, the choices and errors of these stories having too much consequences on these society.

-Albator: Space Pirate, it is an animated serie in which the captain of a pirate team goes on a space journey, to attack and steal space ships. It had a great reputation in the 80’s, for its futuristic aspects.

-Cyborg: An upgraded human, or humanised machine, they are often some manly silhouettes, hiding evil intentions and secret deadly weapons. They have been popularised by movies such as Terminator or Matrix.

-Stargate / Star Trek: Two of the greatest science-fiction sagas, they consist a lot of space and time travel, to encounter aliens, change history and tells versatile stories. These have hugely contributed in the reputation of the modern Science-Fiction.

-Droïd: Robotics being from Star Wars, that have a conscioussness. Often under the orders of a master or an organisation, there is different types of droïds in this universe : service droïds, mechanics or killer droïds, they are part of Star Wars background, it would not be as interesting without them.

-Godzilla: A giant monster, or Kaiju, Godzilla is a giant lizard able to spit laser beams, and destroys skyscrappers. He often fights against other Kaiju to protct himelf, and has once fought King Kong during its fictional life.

-Goldorak:Animated serie in which a giant robot is piloted by a young man in the goal to defend the Earth form aliens. As Albator, it was a great influence of Pop Culture in the 80’s
