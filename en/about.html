About Us

The Creator of this site is Yanis RICHARD, currently present in BUT MMI, in the class of first-year students (C1).

I have been a big Science Fiction fan since I was a kid, and I wanted to show my love for these universes through this site, because they’re a big part of my life, and talking about what I loved will allow me to remember who I was at the time I was writing these words.

The universes of science fiction are a gold mine for those who have a creative and realistic mind at the same time, they allow to credibility an unrealistic idea, by the simple fact of projecting into the future.

This first appeared to me before Back to the Future, and from then on I knew I wanted to contribute to that future in some way. This is the reason why I am studying in a technological field, in order to forge something that is close to my heart, and to leave a trace in a changing world.

These few lines were written in October 2022, if my future self reads to me, I will have only one message to send him: “Don’t give up, your future will be bright, I’m sure of this!”. I think that sums up who I am. Thank you for your interest in this site, sincerely.
